OUR HAPPY STORIES

When a parent dies, the sense of becoming an orphan even as an adult can be overwhelming. People have described feelings such as abandonment, loneliness and anxiety about their future. Here are few happy stories!!

HAPPY PARENTS



Our story starts on 27th September 2001 from this orphanage, from where we adopted one beautiful angel, which changed our life completely. The organisation is very supportive and after completing all the paper work we finally get chance to take our baby girl home. I must thank the organization for their counselling session which helped me and my wife to understand our little girl more. We are glad that we are apart of this organisation.

Madhav


Hey my name is Medha. I and my husband are living happy life just because of Little Angels orphanage, which gave us our little angle in the form of gift. I want to specially thank the staff and for the counselling which taught us about our mental condition and how should we can enhance our parenting skills. I learned a lot from that

Heena


Priya, our little girl which has filled our lives with colours. She makes our life complete. we adopted Priya at early age and have seen her growing up day by day soo beautifully. I want to express my gratitude towards Little Angels which played a huge role in making our lives happy.

Poonam
