LITTLE ANGELS ORPHANAGE

We warmly welcome you to the Little Angels family. Little angels is a home for about 60 abandoned daughters from the age of just eight months to 21-year-old girls, helping them to lead a respectful and happy life. We do not aim to just take care of the basic amenities, but to provide them with every facility to help them figure out what actually makes them happy.
