Why Are We Different!



•  We provide special outing every second weekend to enhance their socializing skills. These outings will help them in improving their confidence in public.


•  We have arranged a proper self-defense class for the girls so that they can defend themselves in situations they shouldn’t be facing. It is not a girl’s fault that she is being forced for something or is being dragged into situations she doesn’t even want to be in. That’s why we have focused more on this self-defense session.


•  Along with overall education, we have different sessions for personality development. We teach them basic etiquettes and how to be confident around people. We teach them to be polite and straightforward so they don’t have trouble while interacting with other people. They are prepared for every kind of situation and know what to do when things get complicated.


•  As we all know mental health of a person is as important as their physical health. But in our society no one wants to talk about mental health of a person and they don’t even bother to know what a person is going through. That’s why we have a special session for our children here. We talk about what they think about something or how are they feeling and try to understand them. They are taught here to express themselves.




Our organization also provide the counselling session for not only parents but also for all the family members who are going to live with child.

One month training will be given to parents with child so that they can eventually learn all the related things about child like dislikes , behaviour regarding different things and etc. this will ensure to have better bonding of parents with child.

Members of organisation will visit to check the family background and the behavior of the all family members if parents are living in joint family. This is to ensure that not only the child be given economic support but also will be provided by good and friendly environment to grow.
