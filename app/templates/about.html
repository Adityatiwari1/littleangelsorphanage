A Family for Every Girl in our Lifetime

Our country India very proudly claims to be living in the motherland and worship various woman deities and goddesses but the dire concern of this is women sadly do not get the same respect and equal status. That why the little angels is A family for every girl who got abandoned by her family got her new family in the form of us. tle angels a family to more than 60 orphaned girls provide them a respectable.
