“Happiness is not the absence of problems, it’s the ability to deal with them.”

We must build orphanages for endangered girls, the street children of India, who beg each day to survive – and often are the prime target of the vicious sex traffickers who prey upon these precious innocents. If you were to come with me to India, especially to the railroad stations and bus stands, you would see these girls. In torn clothing, they beg, hundreds of them, and sadly most of their money goes to the “beggar mafia” or to unscrupulous adults, who use these girls to support their own drug or alcohol habits. Sadly some of these young girls are forced to become prostitutes; we do not want one more lost to such a horrible life. Here are some videos of it.
